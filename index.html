Priyama Majumdar

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the University of Warwick.

My primary fields are political economy and development economics. My secondary interests include the economics of gender and labour economics.

I use experimental and observational methods, complemented by techniques from machine learning and natural language processing.

My current research focuses on contexts in India, Denmark, and the United Kingdom.
