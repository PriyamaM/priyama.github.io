Priyama Majumdar

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the University of Warwick. I will join Princeton University as a postdoctoral researcher in September 2026.

My primary fields are Political Economy and Development Economics, with secondary interests in the Economics of Gender and Labour Economics.

My research combines experimental and observational methods with tools from machine learning and natural language processing. I currently work on projects in India, Denmark, and the United Kingdom.
